# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive educational platform for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This book covers essential topics from ROS 2 fundamentals to advanced Vision Language Action (VLA) models, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical applications.

## What You'll Learn

- ROS 2 fundamentals and advanced concepts
- Simulation environments with Gazebo and Unity
- NVIDIA Isaac platform integration
- Vision Language Action (VLA) models
- Capstone projects implementing learned concepts
